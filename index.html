First Light

Disunification, Consciousness, and the Architecture of Reality

First Light introduces a structural framework for understanding how universes, minds, and identities become possible under constraint.

The work develops disunification as a generative principle; consciousness as an allowed downstream state; and coherence as the condition that permits persistence without appeal to metaphysics, ideology, or belief.

This site exists as a stable reference surface for the framework introduced in the book.
